Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to participate?
Teams must have 4 members (plus an optional substitute). All players must be at least 16 years old and have valid BGMI accounts. Teams must join our Discord server for match coordination.
Is there a registration fee?
Weekly scrims are completely FREE to join! We occasionally host premium tournaments with entry fees that offer larger prize pools.
How are the matches conducted?
Matches are conducted through custom rooms in BGMI. Room ID and password will be shared in our Discord server 15 minutes before match time. We use Erangel and Miramar maps in rotation.
How is the prize money distributed?
Prize money is distributed via UPI/Bank transfer within 48 hours of the tournament conclusion. The team leader is responsible for distributing the prize among team members.
Can I change team members after registration?
You can make changes to your roster until registration closes. After that, only the registered substitute can be used. Contact our support team on Discord for roster changes.
